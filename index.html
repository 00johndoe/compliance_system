Research Objectives

  • Analyze and compare Ghana's NCF with ISO/IEC 27002 through detailed control mapping to identify similarities, differences, and gaps.
  • Develop a compliance measurement model and automated web-based system that evaluates organizational alignment with both frameworks.
  • Test, validate, and generate compliance reports with actionable recommendations for improving cybersecurity alignment.

Scoring Methodology

Compliance Score = (Σ(control_score × weight) / Σ(max_score × weight)) × 100

Each control is assessed using a 0-5 Capability Maturity Model scale, weighted by control criticality, then normalized to a 0-100% score.
